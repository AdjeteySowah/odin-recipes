Coconut Mango Smoothie

A coconut mango smoothie is a refreshing tropical delight that combines the creamy sweetness of ripe mangoes with the exotic flavor of coconut. Blended to silky perfection, this smoothie features chunks of fresh mango blended with creamy coconut milk or coconut water, resulting in a luscious and hydrating beverage. The tropical flavors transport the senses to sun-drenched beaches, while the smooth texture refreshes and rejuvenates. Each sip bursts with the vibrant sweetness of mangoes, balanced by the subtle nuttiness of coconut, creating a harmonious fusion of flavors that is both satisfying and invigorating. Whether enjoyed as a nutritious breakfast alternative or a cooling treat on a hot day, a coconut mango smoothie is a delightful way to indulge in the tropical essence of summer.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces mango pulp, cut into pieces (175gr)
  • 8 fluid ounces coconut milk (240ml)
  • 3 fluid ounces 2% Greek yogurt (90ml)
  • 2-4 tablespoons honey, to taste
  • 2-4 ice cubes, crushed
  • Juice from Â½ a lime

Steps

  1. Place all of the Ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Serve and enjoy!
